Prototype notice. Deal Desk Copilot is an independent concept prototype exploring decision-support tooling for point-of-sale merchant financing conversations — built as a product and UX case study, not affiliated with, built for, or endorsed by any lender, card network, or financial institution. It uses mock data and deterministic sample outputs throughout, does not access credit systems, does not make or communicate lending decisions, and does not provide legal, regulatory, or financial advice. Illustrative monthly figures are examples for conversation framing only — not quotes, offers, or terms. In a production setting, all customer-facing language would require merchant review, and promotional financing claims would need compliance-approved language.
